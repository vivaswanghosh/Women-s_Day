## Uploading Your First Product

### Step 1: Go to Dashboard
Once logged in, click **"Dashboard"** from the navigation menu.

### Step 2: Upload Product Image
1. Click the **image upload area** or **"Click to upload"** button
2. Select a clear, well-lit photo of your handmade product
3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF

**Tip:** Better image quality = Better AI analysis = More accurate pricing

### Step 3: Fill Product Details
- **Product Name:** (Required) E.g., "Hand-stitched Floral Embroidery"
- **Description:** (Optional) Describe your product, materials, size, etc.
- **Material Cost (₹):** How much you spent on materials
- **Hours Spent:** Approximate hours you spent making it
- **Category:** Choose category that matches your product

### Step 4: Get Smart Pricing
Click **"Get Smart Pricing"** button.

Our AI will:
1. Analyze your product image
2. Find 10-20 similar products in the market
3. Calculate optimal price range
4. Suggest a price with confidence score

### Result Display
You'll see:
- **Market Analysis:** Min, Max, and Median prices of similar products
- **AI Suggested Price:** The recommended price (₹) with confidence percentage
- **Similar Products:** List of comparable items the AI found

## Understanding Your Pricing

### Price Components

#### Market Price Range
- **Minimum Price (₹):** Lowest price for similar products
- **Maximum Price (₹):** Highest price for similar products
- **Median Price (₹):** Middle price (most common price point)

#### AI Suggestion
- Shows the optimal price based on market analysis
- **Confidence Score:** 0-100% (higher = more reliable)

### Price Fairness Meter

Once a product is uploaded, you can view its **"Fairness"** status:

- 🟢 **FAIR** - Your price is reasonable (within 25% of market median)
- 🟡 **UNDERPRICED** - You could potentially increase price (more than 25% below median)
- 🔴 **OVERPRICED** - Consider reducing price to match market (more than 25% above median)

## Understanding Profit Metrics

### Cost Price
Amount you spent on materials and supplies.

### Selling Price
Price you set for the product.

### Profit
Profit = Selling Price - Cost Price

Example: If cost is ₹200 and you sell for ₹900, profit is ₹700.

### Profit Margin %
(Profit ÷ Cost) × 100

Example: (700 ÷ 200) × 100 = 350%

Higher margin = better profitability

### Profit Per Hour
Profit ÷ Hours Spent

Shows how much you earn per hour of work.

### ROI (Return on Investment)
(Profit ÷ Cost) × 100

Shows your return on invested money.

## Pricing Tips & Best Practices

### 1. Research Your Market
- Upload products in the same category
- Compare with competitors
- Analyze trending prices

### 2. Consider Your Costs
- Material costs must be covered
- Factor in time (your hourly rate)
- Don't undervalue your work

### 3. Factor in Effort
- Products taking more time = higher price
- Complex designs = more value
- Use the "Hours Spent" field

### 4. Test Different Prices
- Start with AI suggestion
- Monitor sales
- Adjust gradually
- Track profit metrics

### 5. Seasonal Trends
- Prices may vary by season
- Festivals might increase demand
- Check yearly trends

### 6. Don't Underprice
- Underpricing = Less profit
- Low prices may indicate low quality to buyers
- Your work has value

### 7. Regular Updates
- Review prices monthly
- Update based on market changes
- Adjust for season/trends

- Ensure product image is clear
- Check product category

- Describe product better
