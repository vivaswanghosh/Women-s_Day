# CraftLens AI Pitch Deck Presentation Brief

## One-Line Pitch
"CraftLens AI turns handmade creativity into intelligent, data-backed business decisions using computer vision and smart pricing analytics."

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
CraftLens AI is an image-first AI marketplace for handmade creators—embroidery artists, crocheters, and DIY crafters. Users upload a photo of their product, and our AI analyzes thousands of similar items to suggest optimal pricing instantly. We solve the pricing guesswork problem affecting millions of micro-entrepreneurs, especially women-led home businesses.

## 5-Slide Presentation Structure

### Slide 1: Problem Statement
**Title:** "The Pricing Dilemma"

- Millions of hobby creators struggle with pricing decisions
- Current situation: Pricing is guesswork
  - ❌ Underprice → Lose profit
  - ❌ Overprice → Lose sales
- No access to market analytics
- Marketplaces like Etsy/Amazon don't provide pricing help
- **Impact:** Financial loss and lost business potential

### Slide 2: Solution & How It Works
**Title:** "Smart Pricing in 5 Steps"

1. 📸 **Upload Image** - User takes a photo (mandatory)
2. 🧠 **AI Analysis** - Computer vision finds similar products
3. 📊 **Market Research** - Analyzes price distribution
4. 💰 **Smart Suggestion** - AI recommends optimal price + confidence score
5. ✏️ **Control** - User accepts or adjusts anytime

**Key Features:**
- Price Fairness Meter (Underpriced | Fair | Overpriced)
- Profit Margin Calculator (cost + hours → ROI%)
- Trend Intelligence (seasonal patterns, color trends)
- Side-by-side competitor comparison

### Slide 3: Target Market & Revenue Model
**Title:** "Market Opportunity & Business Model"

**Target Users:**
- 🧵 Embroidery artists
- 🧶 Crochet makers
- 💍 Jewelry creators
- 👩‍👧 Women-led micro businesses

**Market Size:** Multi-billion dollar handmade/craft market

**Revenue Streams:**
1. **Freemium:** 5 uploads/month (free)
2. **Pro Subscription:** ₹299/month → unlimited uploads + advanced analytics
3. **Commission:** 5-10% cut on marketplace sales
4. **Premium Features:** Enhanced analytics, featured listings

5. **Show traction:** Current user growth, beta results, testimonials
6. **Close strong:** One-liner + call to action
